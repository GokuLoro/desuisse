Lendi is our highly skilled ring and band designer, whose knowledge in this area is truly impressive. With a career spanning 19 years at our firm, starting back in December 2005, Lendi has been a key figure, showing unwavering loyalty and dedication. Over the years, he has attended numerous exhibitions in Turkey, constantly striving to improve his craft and keep up with the latest trends in ring and band design. His expertise makes him an invaluable asset to the team.

Lendi is also a passionate sports fan, dividing his time between his two favorite sports: football and basketball. As a die-hard Chelsea FC supporter, he watches every game he can. His most cherished memory as a fan is the iconic 2012 Champions League victory, a moment he recalls fondly with legends like Didier Drogba and Frank Lampard leading the charge. Basketball, too, plays a big role in Lendi's life, as he faithfully supports his hometown team, KB Peja. Not only does he attend every home game, but he also travels to some of the more important away matches to show his support..

Despite his busy schedule, Lendi always finds time for his personal fitness. His commitment to staying active and working out regularly is an essential part of his routine, no matter how packed his day gets.
